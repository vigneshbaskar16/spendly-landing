## 🎯 Objective

To build a personal expense tracker that helps users log, categorize, and visualize their daily spending — with zero friction and maximum clarity.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📊 Smart expense & income tracking
- 🗂️ Custom categories with icons
- 📷 OCR bill scanning (point & log)
- 📈 Visual charts — bar & donut
- ☁️ Cloud sync across devices
- 🔒 AES-256 encryption + biometric lock
- 📤 PDF & CSV export
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Family shared budgets (up to 5 members)
